Licensing for Teachers

Musicianship Trainer licensing is provided at no cost to NZMEB teachers. In order to obtain your free license please email sales@musicmastereducation.co.nz. We will reply email to you with the license instructions.

Licensing for Students

Musicianship Trainer licenses are purchased as In-App-Purchases on the App Store within the app. The license cost is listed in the app. The license enables all content and exams for the specific NZMEB Grade you purchase. Licenses are valid for the current calendar year.

Download Musicianship Trainer from the App Store as shown above. Then use the Licenses icon to purchase the license for your Grade. The Licenses icon is shown on the header page of each Grade.
