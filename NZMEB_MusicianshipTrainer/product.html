Musicianship Trainer

Musicianship Trainer is a new interactive app designed to significantly reduce the workload of music teachers who use the NZMEB exam syllabus and to enhance the musicianship skills of their students.

• NZMEB endorsed and available on the NZMEB website • Currently available for Grades 1, 2 and 3 piano on Apple iPad and iPhone • Practice Mode includes practice tips, many examples and feedback • Exam Mode includes a large number of mock exams delivered in the style of your on-the-day examiner • Instructions and student video demonstrations for two age brackets: 5-10 years (with parent help sections) and 11-plus years • Free licensing for NZMEB teachers
